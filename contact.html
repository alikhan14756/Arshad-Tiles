FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Shabqadar and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on the distance and order size. Contact us for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments. For large orders, we also offer installment plans. Please inquire at our showroom for more information.

Do you provide installation services?

While we primarily supply products, we can recommend trusted installers who have worked with our materials. We ensure you get professional installation services.

Can I get samples before purchasing?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to take samples home to see how they look in their space. Visit our showroom to collect samples of your preferred products.
