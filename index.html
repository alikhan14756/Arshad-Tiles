About Us

12+ Years of Excellence in Tile and Sanitary Solutions

At Arshad Tiles and Sanitary, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality products and exceptional customer service. Located in the heart of Shabqadar Bazar near Flying Coach Adda, we have been serving our community since 2012 with the finest selection of tiles, sanitary ware, and PVC wall panels.

Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and expert guidance has made us the trusted choice for homeowners, contractors, and builders in the region. Whether you are renovating your home or working on a large commercial project, we have everything you need under one roof.
